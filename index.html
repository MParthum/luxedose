Botox

Botox, Dysport, Xeomin,& Jeuveau are a type of Botulinum Type A used to relax overactive muscles of the face. There are multiple treatment options for all four types of Botox, including: Migraines, Forehead Lines, Frown Lines, Glabellar Lines, Eyebrow Lifts, Crows Feet, Under Eye Lines, Bunny Lines, Gummy Smile, Bruxism, Lip Lines, Marionette Lines/Prejowl, Pitted/Orange Skin, Platysmal Bands, Neck Rings, and Hyperhidrosis.

All About The Luxe Dose

Owner and master trained injector Bianca Paraison RN, BSN has 10 years experience as a Registered Nurse in a Boston teaching hospital. After specializing in ear, nose, and throat, oral maxillofacial surgery, neurosurgery and cardiac surgery, she broadened her scope of practice to Aesthetics. After training with Dr. Micheal Lum of the Esthetics Skin Institute as well as exstensive training with industry leaders Galderma and Allergan she has brought her advanced nursing and aesthetics skills to her very own practice in Belmont, MA. Here at The Luxe Dose, she specializes in botox, dermal filler, body contouring, and medical grade skincare. Consults for injectable's are free and online skincare consults are now available!
